# Facebook Business Manager Setup for Nxt Leads

## OBJECTIVE: $20K+ Daily Ad Spend Capability

### Phase 1: Business Manager Accounts Setup

**Required: 3 Separate Facebook Business Manager Accounts**
- **Account 1**: Primary ($10K/day target)
- **Account 2**: Secondary ($7K/day target) 
- **Account 3**: Backup/Scale ($5K/day target)

### Business Manager Creation Checklist

**Per Account Requirements:**
- [ ] Unique business email address
- [ ] Verified phone number
- [ ] Business website (nxtleads.com or variations)
- [ ] Business address (can use virtual office)
- [ ] Business tax ID/EIN
- [ ] Business bank account
- [ ] Business credit card (high limit preferred)

### Account Setup Process

**Step 1: Create Business Manager**
1. Go to business.facebook.com
2. Create Business Manager with unique email
3. Verify business information
4. Add business details (address, phone, website)

**Step 2: Add Payment Method**
1. Add business credit card to account
2. Set high spending limits ($5K-$10K/day)
3. Verify payment method
4. Set up automatic billing

**Step 3: Create Ad Account**
1. Create new ad account within Business Manager
2. Set country and currency (USD)
3. Set business purpose: Lead Generation
4. Add payment method to ad account

**Step 4: Verify Business**
1. Submit business verification documents
2. Upload business license/registration
3. Provide utility bill or bank statement
4. Wait for verification (1-3 days typically)

### Pixel Installation

**Meta Pixel Setup:**
1. Create Meta Pixel in Events Manager
2. Install pixel on nxtleads.com website
3. Set up conversion tracking for:
   - Page views
   - Lead form submissions
   - Lead purchases
   - Account registrations

**Conversion Events Priority:**
- **Primary**: Lead Purchase Complete
- **Secondary**: Lead Form Submit
- **Tertiary**: Account Registration
- **Monitoring**: Page View

### Campaign Structure Planning

**Campaign Types:**
1. **Prospecting Campaigns** (60% of budget)
   - Interest-based targeting
   - Lookalike audiences
   - Broad targeting with Meta AI

2. **Retargeting Campaigns** (25% of budget)
   - Website visitors
   - Video watchers
   - Lead form abandoners

3. **Custom Audience Campaigns** (15% of budget)
   - Uploaded customer lists
   - Lookalike from existing customers

### Creative Requirements

**Ad Creative Specs:**
- **Images**: 1200x628px (landscape), 1080x1080px (square)
- **Videos**: 1080x1920px (stories), 1200x628px (feed)
- **Carousel**: 1080x1080px per image
- **Text**: Under 20% of image area

**Creative Themes for Life Insurance Leads:**
1. **Family Protection** - "Protect Your Family's Future"
2. **Financial Security** - "Secure Your Family's Financial Future"
3. **Peace of Mind** - "Sleep Better Knowing Your Family is Protected"
4. **Income Replacement** - "Replace Your Income if Something Happens"

### Landing Page Integration

**Required Landing Pages:**
- `/facebook-lead-iul` - IUL insurance landing page
- `/facebook-lead-mortgage` - Mortgage protection landing page
- `/facebook-lead-final` - Final expense landing page
- `/facebook-lead-trucker` - Trucker insurance landing page

**Landing Page Must-Haves:**
- [ ] Meta Pixel firing correctly
- [ ] Lead form with phone number
- [ ] TCPA compliance checkbox
- [ ] "Thank you" page with conversion pixel
- [ ] Mobile-optimized forms
- [ ] SSL certificate
- [ ] Fast loading (under 2 seconds)

### Compliance Requirements

**TCPA Compliance:**
- Clear opt-in language
- Phone number collection with consent
- Record of consent timestamp
- Easy opt-out mechanism

**Facebook Policy Compliance:**
- No misleading claims about guaranteed coverage
- No fake urgency or false scarcity
- Clear and honest value proposition
- Professional, trustworthy creative

### Daily Management Workflow

**Daily Tasks:**
1. Check account health and spending
2. Review cost per lead performance
3. Pause underperforming ads
4. Scale winning campaigns
5. Upload new creatives
6. Check for policy violations

**Weekly Tasks:**
1. Analyze conversion data
2. Build new lookalike audiences
3. Create new ad creative
4. Adjust targeting based on performance
5. Review and optimize landing pages

### Budget Scaling Strategy

**Week 1**: $500/day total (testing)
**Week 2**: $1,000/day (winning campaigns)
**Week 3**: $2,500/day (proven funnels)
**Week 4**: $5,000/day (scale mode)
**Month 2**: $10,000+/day (full scale)

### Key Performance Indicators

**Success Metrics:**
- **Cost per Lead**: Under $30
- **Lead-to-Sale Rate**: Above 15%
- **Customer Lifetime Value**: Above $300
- **Return on Ad Spend**: Above 3:1

**Warning Signals:**
- CPL above $40
- Conversion rate below 10%
- Account restrictions/warnings
- Spending not meeting daily budget

### Account Backup Strategy

**If Account Gets Restricted:**
1. Immediately pause all campaigns
2. Submit appeal with business documents
3. Activate backup Business Manager
4. Redirect traffic to backup account
5. Continue operations without interruption

**Risk Mitigation:**
- Never run same creative on multiple accounts
- Use different landing page URLs per account
- Stagger campaign launches across accounts
- Maintain different payment methods per account
